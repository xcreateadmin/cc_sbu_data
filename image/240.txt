The image shows a gearbox, which is a mechanical device used to transmit power from one rotating shaft to another. The gearbox consists of several gears and shafts that work together to change the speed and torque of the rotating shafts. The gears are made of metal and have teeth that mesh with each other to transmit power. The shafts are used to hold the gears in place. The gearbox is used in automobiles, motorcycles, and industrial machinery.
