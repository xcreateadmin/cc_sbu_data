The image is a stylized representation of the constellation Scorpius, which is located between Libra and Sagittarius in the zodiac. Scorpius is one of the largest and most recognizable constellations in the night sky due to its distinctive scorpion-like shape. Antares, a red supergiant, is the brightest star in Scorpius and can be seen with the naked eye. Other bright stars in Scorpius include Delta Scorpii and Lambda Scorpii. The constellation is often associated with the astrological sign of Scorpio and depicted as a scorpion in art and mythology.
