This is a dish of chicken tikka masala, which is a popular Indian dish made with chicken, tomatoes, onions, and a variety of spices. The chicken is marinated in a mixture of yogurt, spices, and lemon juice before being grilled or baked in the oven. The sauce is made with tomatoes, onions, ginger, garlic, and a variety of spices, including cumin, coriander, and turmeric. The dish is typically served with rice or naan bread and garnished with fresh cilantro.
