Transformers Dark of the Moon is a video game based on the 2011 film of the same name. The game follows the story of the Autobots and Decepticons as they battle for control of Earth. Players can choose to play as either the Autobots or Decepticons, each with their own unique abilities and weapons. The game also features a multiplayer mode, allowing players to compete against each other in various game modes. It was developed by High Moon Studios and published by Activision.
