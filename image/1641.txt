This is a map that shows the location of the ancient city of Babylon in Mesopotamia. Babylon was the capital of the Babylonian Empire and known for its impressive architecture, including the famous Hanging Gardens and the Ishtar Gate. It was also significant religiously, with many important temples and shrines. The map shows Babylon's location in relation to other important cities in Mesopotamia, such as Assyria and Akkad, and the Euphrates River, which flowed through Babylon and was an important source of water for the city.
