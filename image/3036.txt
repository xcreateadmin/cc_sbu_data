The Jeep Compass is a compact SUV produced by Jeep. It was introduced in 2017 and is in its second generation. It offers a range of engines, including a 2.4 liter four cylinder and a 1.4 liter turbocharged four cylinder, both paired with a six speed automatic transmission. The Compass seats five passengers and has a cargo capacity of up to 63.3 cubic feet with the rear seats folded down. It is available in several trim levels, including the Sport, Latitude, Limited, and Trailhawk. The Compass is known for its off road capabilities and is often used for outdoor activities.
